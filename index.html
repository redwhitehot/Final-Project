SAFEHOUSE

ABOUT
Safehouse is an experiential game of trade-offs for students.

Players experience first-hand key investing principles such as risk diversification and Value versus Price.

As a participant, how can you preserve and grow your limited resources while navigating an uncertain terrain that is fraught with danger?

Do you have what it takes to survive and thrive?

PLAY
'The Last Reserve' is the online version of Safehouse.

In this 15-episode mobile-enabled online strategy game of trade-offs, you lead a tribe of survivors and need to make life-and-death decisions everyday.

Win exclusive badges and compete against your friends to see who has what it takes to be the Ultimate Survivor.
